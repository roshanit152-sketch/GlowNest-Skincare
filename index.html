Our Journey

Rooted in Wellness, Based in Butwal

OUR BUTWAL ATELIER

Bringing Global Skincare Excellence to the Heart of Lumbini.

GlowNest Skincare was born out of a localized necessity in Butwal. The unique weather profile of our region—transitioning from scorching, humid plains summers into dry sub-tropical winters—demands flexible skin dynamic management.

We realized that modern Nepalese consumers were often trapped between heavily greasy traditional options or imported global formulas that failed to factor in local environmental stresses.

Our mandate is selective curation. We bridge the absolute zenith of global formulations—highly coveted multi-layered Korean skin toners and lightweight Japanese UV blockades—with natural high-altitude Nepalese herb extractions. Every product in our boutique storefront is guaranteed authentic, clean, and optimized for local complexions.
